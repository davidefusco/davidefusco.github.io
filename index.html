aboutme

I'm a former hospitality professional and business owner turned UX Designer. The CareerFoundry UX course has inspired me to bring my passion for understanding and satisfying customer needs into the digital world. My portfolio focuses on responsible design and extensive research; in the first I built some key features for a mobile app for learning new vocabulary and in the second I explored the tattoo world and built a high fidelity prototype for the mobile app, Inktastic.
